About SASE Regionals

The 2021 SASE West Regional Conference is an event which invites company speakers to host professional development workshops and provides networking opportunities to members across the West Region.

The 2021 West Regional Conference theme — "Adapting Amidst Adversity" — aims to inspire students to motivate themselves and continue succeeding to their maximum potential, despite the challenges posed by the pandemic. The event will not only prepare students for success for their careers, but also provide a hand to the community through local charities and service.
