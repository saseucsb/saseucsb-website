About SASE Regionals

The SASE West Regional Conference at UCSB aims to connect different chapters of SASE from all across the west coast. Twenty-one active chapters in the western region will be present along with a slew of local and national companies, with attendees engaging in bonding activities, professional workshops, and networking opportunities.

One of the main highlights of the conference will be the series of workshops and panels focusing on leadership, technology and entrepreneurship, career development, diversity and inclusion, or social connection. Sponsoring companies will be able to act as hosts for these events, choosing among the many workshop ideas we will provide or potentially bringing their own ideas to educate invested students about various concepts. During the opening ceremony and closing remarks we will also bring in executives and key speakers from the SASE national organization, our platinum sponsor, or other external sources to provide advice and new perspectives. Multiple large scale networking sessions will also be held during the conference, giving students additional chances to individually connect with employers and explore different available opportunities in their field.

Our SASE West Regional Conference’s theme, “Adapting Amidst Adversity”, reflects back on a year of hardships and unexpected change, and inspires all of us to recover and move forward with our lives. To help those outside of our conference community, we will also have a community service opportunity for interested attendees with the local PATH homeless shelter that we routinely work with. Even with a fully virtual conference, we will provide avenues for attendees to donate to PATH and to other non-profits like Direct Relief (for COVID-19 support) by participating in our raffles and purchasing conference merchandise.

Platform

SASE UCSB has spent a great deal of time looking into the special circumstances our attendees are subject to and as such, has decided to host an entirely virtual conference. It is unfortunate that our beautiful UCSB campus will not act as the conference location, but SASE Nationals has been able extend their use of a high-quality virtual platform called Socio to the regional conferences. This highly customizable, fully functional platform includes a plethora of capabilities to maximize the engagement of our attendees and ensure they are able to connect with even more people than before. We will be providing extra resources to all attending companies about using these capabilities and more details are also available on their website (https://socio.events).
